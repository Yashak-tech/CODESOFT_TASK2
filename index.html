FAQ

Good to know

How fast will I know if something goes down?

Most outages are detected and alerted on within 60 seconds, thanks to checks from multiple regions confirming the failure before paging you.

Can I monitor things that aren't websites?

Yes — Pulse supports HTTP(S), TCP ports, ping, DNS records, SSL certificate expiry, and keyword checks on page content.

Do you offer a free plan?

Yes. The Starter plan is free forever and includes 5 monitors with 5-minute checks — plenty for personal projects.

Can my customers see our status page?

Every Team and Scale plan includes a branded public status page you can host on your own subdomain.
